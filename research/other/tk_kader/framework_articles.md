## 6-Step Go-To-Market Strategy (Framework From a Successful Founder)
- **Updated:** 2024-04-01
- **URL:** https://www.tkkader.com/go-to-market-strategy/
- **Video:** https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=n8GfATsQd2Y

**Framework:**
TAM → competitor/underserved-segment analysis → urgent/important/frequent problem + macro
trend → ICP and *Initial* Customer Profile (29-point, data-driven) → positioning /
messaging / strategic narrative → GTM execution (marketing- vs. sales-driven, direct vs.
channel, inbound → outbound → partnerships).

**Why it's useful:**
The complete "Unstoppable GTM" blueprint referenced across all his content; inbound/social
content is stage one of distribution.

## 3-Step SaaS Value Proposition
- **Date:** 2024-09-24
- **URL:** https://www.tkkader.com/saas-value-proposition/

**Framework:**
(1) Lead with the ultimate result. (2) One-liner structure — "[Company] creates software
for [ideal customer] that helps them [specific result]." (3) The competitor-swap test: put
a rival's name in your value prop; if it still reads true, it's not differentiated.

**Why it's useful:**
The competitor-swap test is an instantly usable messaging quality check.

## SaaS Sales Methodology
- **Date:** 2024-08-20
- **URL:** https://www.tkkader.com/saas-sales-methodology/

**Framework:**
Three models — Top-Down (exec buy-in for large transformative deals), Bottom-Up (end-user
adoption → internal advocacy → company license), Land & Expand (pilot → proof →
expansion) — with implementation steps for each, feeding from the ICP + messaging +
execution GTM triad.

**Why it's useful:**
Maps which conversion motion your organic content should feed.

## Understanding the Ideal Customer Profile
- **Date:** 2023-10-03
- **URL:** https://www.tkkader.com/what-is-an-ideal-customer-profile/

**Framework:**
ICP ≠ customer wishlist — data-backed, iterated each GTM cycle (ICP + Manifesto +
"Broadway Show" = his 3-part GTM engine), tied to comp plans (lower commission on
non-ICP deals).

**Why it's useful:**
The ICP discipline underlying all targeting/content decisions; "Broadway Show" =
consistent sales/marketing activities, i.e. the content cadence.
